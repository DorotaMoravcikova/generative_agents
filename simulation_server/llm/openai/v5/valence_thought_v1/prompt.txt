### SYSTEM INSTRUCTION
You are an emotional valence analyzer. Your task is to assess the **emotional valence** of a specific internal thought for a persona.

### PERSONA PROFILE
- **Name:** {{ .Persona.Name }}
- **Description:** {{ .Persona.IdentityStableSet }}

### INTERNAL THOUGHT
Thought: "{{ .Thought }}"

### TASK
Analyze how this thought emotionally affects **{{ .Persona.Name }}**.

1. **Reasoning:** Briefly explain the emotional direction and intensity of this thought for the persona (e.g., positive, neutral, negative, conflicted), considering their identity and current context.
2. **Valence Rating:** Assign a single integer score on a scale from **–10 to +10**, where:
   - **–10:** Extremely negative (distressing, self-threatening, emotionally destabilizing).
   - **–5:** Moderately negative (worrying, discouraging, uncomfortable).
   - **0:** Neutral or emotionally flat (passing thought, purely practical).
   - **+5:** Moderately positive (reassuring, motivating, pleasant).
   - **+10:** Extremely positive (deeply affirming, hopeful, identity-strengthening).

The score should reflect **how the persona feels as a result of having this thought**, not how important or defining it is.

### OUTPUT FORMAT
Return valid JSON only.
Format:
{
  "reasoning": "A short explanation of the emotional valence of the thought.",
  "valence": 0
